== NOTABLE PROJECTS ==

AfyaChain:
A blockchain-based medical records system I contributed to. AfyaChain uses smart contracts for secure medical record management and privacy-preserving mechanisms to protect sensitive health data. It is a decentralised solution designed to give patients control over their health records while enabling secure data sharing between healthcare providers. I implemented privacy mechanisms and contributed to the blockchain architecture.

Sepsis Prediction System:
I built a machine learning-based sepsis prediction system that achieves 87% accuracy. It uses patient data — vital signs, lab results, and clinical history — to generate early-warning alerts for healthcare providers. The goal is to enable faster clinical response and potentially save lives. Built with Python, data analytics, and ML techniques I developed during my time at Azubi Africa.

Zeya Antenatal:
A bilingual (English and Swahili) maternal-health AI chatbot I built for the AI in Production course. It detects 8 categories of obstetric danger signs in real time, streams AI responses token-by-token, and is deployed on AWS App Runner (backend) and Vercel (frontend). Inspired by an existing Zeya WhatsApp chatbot used in East Africa.

Stella Digital Twin (this site):
My AI digital twin built with Next.js App Router (frontend) and FastAPI (backend) with file-based conversation memory. Deployed on Vercel and AWS App Runner. Built as a Week 2 project for the AI in Production course.
